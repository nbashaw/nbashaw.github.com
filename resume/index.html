About Me

Ever since middle school, when I got my first bootlegged copy of Photoshop, Iâ€™ve been fascinated with making things for the web. It started out with logos, and by college progresed into web-design. Sophomore year I volunteered to make the website for my student organization (Model UN), and junior year I made an app with friends called Thoughtback that ended up attracting over 25 thousand users. From then on I was hooked.

Since college, I've expanded my skillset from basic HTML and CSS to include Javascript, UX/UI design, and also back-end technologies like Ruby, Rails, Sinatra, Git, and MongoDB. I love the process of taking ideas from conversation to polished product. I believe in perfecting every detail, but limiting the number of details to perfect. (Thanks for the mantra, Jack).
